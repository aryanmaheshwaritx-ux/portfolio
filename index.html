Founder & CEO Patent Pending May 2025 – Present

Drone-Based Wind Turbine Sensor Deployment

Aero Robotics builds robotic systems that automate dangerous tasks in harsh environments. The company's first project is a drone-deployed payload that places sensors directly on wind turbine blades — eliminating the need for technicians to work at dangerous heights. The system handles surface preparation, composite-safe cleaning, resin tape adhesion, and precision sensor placement, all within strict drone payload weight limits.

I designed and built the full system: mechanical payload design in Fusion 360, flight stack configuration on ArduPilot and Pixhawk, field integration testing, and iteration through real deployment trials. The work earned a USPTO provisional patent application in 2025. Every design decision was shaped by what actually works in the field — not just on a bench.

USPTO Application 63/883,099 — Filed September 15, 2025 · Co-inventor: Aleksey Jarvich
